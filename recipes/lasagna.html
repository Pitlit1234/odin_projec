Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and marinara sauce. It's a hearty meal that can be enjoyed by the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef and onion in a pan until browned.
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  4. Layer the noodles, ricotta cheese, beef mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
