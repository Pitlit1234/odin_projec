Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, cheese, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 12 soft taco shells
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef and add taco seasoning according to package instructions.
  2. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  3. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with beef, lettuce, cheese, and salsa.
